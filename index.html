The Opportunity

A Significant Value Gap
in Fermentation Industries

Fermentation industries generate substantial volumes of co-product material that are routinely undervalued. A new class of biorefinery technology is changing that equation.

01

Undervalued Co-Products

Fermentation industries generate significant volumes of microbial co-products that are routinely discarded or sold at commodity prices. Meanwhile, Canada imports the majority of its premium functional food and nutraceutical ingredients — a gap that domestic valorization can close.

02

Structural Value Lost

Conventional recovery methods sacrifice the structural integrity of the most commercially valuable components, limiting their application in premium food, nutraceutical, and functional ingredient markets.

03

A Better Pathway

A new generation of gentle biorefinery processing unlocks multiple high-value ingredient categories simultaneously from a single feedstock — with proprietary methods, scalable infrastructure, and a consortium-ready deployment model.

Our Platform

Proprietary Processing
Platform

A proprietary platform technology that converts fermentation co-product streams into multiple distinct, market-ready ingredient categories. Patent pending. Designed from the outset for collaborative, consortium-based deployment.

01

Canadian Ingredient Supply Chain

Canada imports the majority of the premium nutraceutical and functional food ingredients it consumes. Levurix's platform recovers these ingredient categories domestically — from Canadian fermentation industry co-product streams — reducing import dependence and establishing a new domestic supply chain.

02

Built for Consortium Deployment

The platform is designed for collaborative deployment with fermentation industry feedstock partners, ingredient offtakers, and food manufacturers. Modular and scalable, it is suited to consortium-based co-investment from pilot through industrial scale — reducing capital risk for all partners.

03

Multi-Stream Valorization

A single co-product input yields multiple distinct, market-ready ingredient categories — each with preserved native functional properties. This multi-stream recovery model maximises economic value per kilogram of feedstock, delivering outcomes that single-product approaches cannot replicate.

Sustainability & ESG

Built to Reduce.
Designed to Replace.

Levurix was designed around circular economy principles from the ground up. By redirecting organic-rich co-product streams that would otherwise be discarded, our platform creates measurable environmental value across waste diversion, carbon displacement, and resource efficiency simultaneously.

Organic Waste Diversion

Co-product streams diverted from wastewater and landfill pathways reduce biological oxygen demand (BOD) loading on municipal treatment infrastructure — measurable, auditable, and directly tied to feedstock volume processed.

Waste Reduction
Low-Carbon Protein

Fermentation-derived protein carries a fraction of the carbon footprint of animal-derived equivalents — no land use change, no feed conversion losses, no enteric methane.

Carbon Displacement
Ingredient Displacement

Each product stream displaces a higher-footprint incumbent — animal-derived protein, synthetically produced nutraceutical actives, and rendered animal meal in pet nutrition.

Circular Inputs
Industrial Symbiosis

One industry's co-product stream becomes another's premium input — with low energy requirements and no virgin resource extraction. Applicable across fermentation sectors: brewing, dairy processing, distilling, and industrial fermentation.
